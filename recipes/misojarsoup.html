Miso Jar Soup

Ingredients

  • 12 ounces firm tofu
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 3 tablespoons white miso paste
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable stock base
  • 1 tablespoon grated fresh ginger
  • 1.5 cups cooked rice vermicelli noodles
  • 1 cup thinly sliced shittake mushrooms
  • 1 cup thinly sliced baby bok choy

Steps

  1. Cut tofu into 0.5 inch plants. Heat oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Cook tofu, turning halfway through, until holden. Cut tofu into cubes

  3. Layer noodles, tofu, mushrooms and boy chill. Chill covered for up to 5 days
