One Pot Spaghetti

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground Italian sausage
  • 1 small white onion, diced
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced/li>
  • 1 (26 ounce) jar tomato-basil pasta sauce
  • 2 cups water
  • 1 teaspoon Italian seasoning
  • 1 (8 ounce) package spaghetti noodles, broken in half
  • Â½ cup freshly grated Parmesan cheese for serving (Optional)

Steps

  1. Combine ground sausage, onions and garlic in a large pot, cook over medium till cooked through drain and discard grease
  2. Stir pasta sauce, water and Italian seasoning into the pot, bring to a boil. Stir in noodles, return to a boil and cook, stir occasionally until al dente
  3. Serve topped with grated Parmesan cheese
